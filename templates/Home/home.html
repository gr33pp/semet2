About SEMET

About

Our goal is to develop a web that empowers users to monitor their energy usage, receive personalized recommendations for energy efficiency improvements. By doing so, the project aims to reduce energy consumption, lower utility bills, and contribute to environmental sustainability in African urban areas.

SEMET FEATURES

01

Energy Usage Monitoring

◦ Integration with smart meters or manual input of energy usage data.

Real-time tracking and historical data analysis of energy consumption.

Efficiency Recommendations

◦ Analysis of usage patterns to suggest improvements, such as using energy-efficient appliances or adjusting usage times to off-peak hours.

◦ Tips on improving building insulation and other energy-saving measures.

03

Gamification:

◦ Challenges and rewards for users who reduce their energy consumption.

◦ Community features for users to share tips and compete in energy-saving challenges.
